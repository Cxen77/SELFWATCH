# Trajectory-First Tracking Notes

Traditional trackers rely heavily on:
- appearance similarity
- nearest-neighbor association

SELFWATCH explores:
- trajectory continuity
- direction consistency
- motion plausibility
- persistent ownership

Core question:

"Could this physically be the same person?"

instead of:

"Does this look visually similar?"

Main ideas:
- direction-aware association
- pseudo-depth reasoning
- ACT-R persistence
- occlusion-aware ownership
- cognitive memory states
