# v0.5 — Trajectory-First Identity Ownership

**Date**: 2026-05
**Git Tag**: `v0.5.0`

## Motivation

Even with phantom tracking and memory, identity assignment still relied heavily on appearance similarity. In crowded scenes with similar-looking individuals, this caused ID switches. Trajectory continuity is a stronger signal for identity persistence.

## What Changed

- Trajectory-aware rebinding: identity ownership follows trajectory direction
- Direction-continuity gating: prevents identity "teleportation" across the scene
- Observation-centric motion gating: validates re-binding against expected motion
- Gait signature integration: biomechanical identity signal
- Scene topology learning: spatial priors from observed patterns

## Impact
