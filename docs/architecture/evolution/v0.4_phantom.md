# v0.4 — Phantom Tracking

**Date**: 2026-05
**Git Tag**: `v0.4.0` (retroactive)

## Motivation

When a person is occluded, the tracker loses them and must rely on warm memory for recovery. Phantom tracking bridges this gap by maintaining a predicted trajectory during occlusion, enabling faster and more accurate identity recovery.

## What Changed

- Phantom tracker: continues motion prediction during occlusion
- Phantom matching: dedicated matching logic when person re-appears
- Contradiction detector: catches duplicate IDs and identity hijacking
- Attention priority: selective ReID based on track stability
- Deferred decision system: delays ambiguous identity assignments

## Impact

| Metric | v0.3 | v0.4 | Delta |
|--------|------|------|-------|
| ID Switches | Moderate | Low | Improved |
| Occlusion Recovery | Slow | Fast | Significantly improved |
| FPS | ~12 | ~12 | Neutral |
